Our Story

Founded in 2021 with a vision to revolutionize beauty and wellness in Nigeria.

MadePetal Clinic and Spa emerged from a simple yet powerful belief: everyone deserves access to premium, scientifically-backed beauty treatments in a luxurious, nurturing environment. What started as a small clinic in Magboro has blossomed into a comprehensive beauty destination serving clients across Lagos, Ogun, and Oyo states.

Our founder, driven by a passion for holistic beauty and wellness, combined extensive clinical expertise with a deep appreciation for organic, natural ingredients. This unique fusion of science and nature became the cornerstone of the MadePetal philosophy.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, personalized beauty and wellness solutions that enhance natural beauty while promoting overall wellbeing. We combine cutting-edge aesthetic technology with organic, sustainable ingredients to deliver transformative results.

We're committed to empowering our clients through education, helping them make informed decisions about their beauty journey while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading destination for integrated beauty and wellness, recognized for our innovative approach that harmonizes clinical expertise with natural, organic solutions.

We envision a future where every individual has access to premium beauty care that not only enhances appearance but also nurtures confidence and promotes lasting wellness from within.
